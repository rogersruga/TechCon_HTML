History

TechCon began as a small community meetup in 2016, formed by a collective of local developers, students, and innovators who shared one goal—creating a space where technology enthusiasts could learn, collaborate, and showcase new ideas. What started as a single-room gathering with a few guest speakers quickly grew into an annual tradition that attracted participants from across the region.


Our Mission

TechCon 2024 is driven by a simple goal: to inspire innovation, support learning, and bring the tech community together. We aim to give students, creators, and professionals a space to explore new ideas, build skills, and connect with others shaping the future of technology.
